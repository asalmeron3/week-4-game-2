Star Wars RPG

Select a character to start the game

There are four characters total. Each character has unique attack point, counter-attack points, and defense points.

To win the game, you must defeat the remaining three (3) characters.

Things to note:
1) When you attack, the current opposing character will use its counter-attack to weaken your defense.
2) Your attack deals more damage after each attack. Ex: 2nd attack deals twice the damage, 3rd attack deals three times the damage, etc.
Counter-Attacks by the opposing character remain the same.
